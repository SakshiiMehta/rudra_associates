Services

Have a Glance At Our Uniquely Procurred Servies all at Your Doorstep

Equity trading allows investors to purchase ownership stakes in publicly-listed companies. At Rudra Associates, we offer efficient, secure, and transparent trading platforms for executing equity trades, tailored for both novice and experienced traders.

Service Features:

1. Research & Analysis: We provide in-depth research reports and stock recommendations based on market analysis, fundamental and technical factors, and future growth projections.
2. Real-Time Trading:Access to real-time trading platforms where clients can execute buy or sell orders instantly with live market updates.
3. Market Insights: Timely market insights, news, and data, enabling investors to make informed decisions.
4. Custom Alerts: Set up real-time alerts for specific stocks, price movements, or trends, ensuring you don’t miss critical opportunities.

We ensure seamless trading experiences by providing direct access to the National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) for executing trades.

Derivatives are financial instruments whose value is derived from an underlying asset. Our Derivatives Trading Services cover a broad range of products, including futures, options, and other advanced strategies that help investors manage risk or speculate in different markets.

Service Features:

1. Futures Contracts: Trade in future contracts for equities, indices, or commodities, allowing investors to lock in prices at present to buy or sell in the future.
2. Options Trading: Buy or sell options for better flexibility in protecting your investments and taking advantage of price fluctuations.
3. Margin Trading: Access to leverage by trading on margin, enabling larger market exposure with a lower upfront investment.
4. Hedging Services: Tailored strategies to minimize risk exposure for asset-heavy investors or businesses dealing with volatile markets.

Our team provides comprehensive risk management solutions through derivatives, helping clients protect their portfolios during market volatility.

Commodity trading involves buying and selling raw materials like metals, agricultural products, and energy sources. Rudra Associates gives you direct access to national and international commodity markets to diversify your portfolio and hedge against inflation.

Service Features:

1. Commodity Futures Trading: Investors can trade futures contracts for commodities like gold, crude oil, agricultural products, and metals, allowing speculation on future price movements.
2. Spot Trading: Buy and sell commodities at current market prices with immediate settlement.
3. Comprehensive Market Research: Detailed reports and analysis of commodity prices, tr ends, and market outlook to help you make informed decisions.
4. Multinational Exposure: Access to global markets for commodities, giving investors the opportunity to trade with a diverse range of international contracts.

Our expert advisory team provides real-time updates, risk management strategies, and market trend analysis for commodity investors.

Currency futures involve buying or selling a currency at a set price in the future. These are crucial for businesses and investors involved in international trade, helping mitigate currency risk and capitalize on foreign exchange fluctuations.

Service Features:

1. Forex Market Access: Trade in major currencies such as USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, and INR on global exchanges.
2. Hedging Solutions: Use currency futures to protect against adverse price movements in foreign exchange, essential for importers, exporters, and global traders.
3. Leverage Opportunities: Benefit from leverage by trading currency futures contracts, which allow you to control large amounts of currency with a relatively small investment.
4. Advanced Charting Tools: Access to live charts and trading tools, helping you identify key forex market trends and movements in real time.

Rudra Associates offers a range of currency-related services that help businesses and investors mitigate risks associated with currency fluctuations and global trade.

Depository services involve the electronic holding of securities like shares, bonds, and mutual funds. Rudra Associates facilitates easy, secure, and efficient management of your investments by providing services like dematerialization (Demat) accounts for electronic holdings.

Service Features:

1. Demat Accounts: Secure, paperless storage of your securities. We offer easy and hassle-free account opening and management.
2. Electronic Transfers: Transfer securities between accounts and execute buy/sell transactions without the need for physical certificates.
3. Statement Management: Automated and real-time access to transaction statements, corporate actions, and tax-related documents.
4. Security & Compliance: Our depository services comply with stringent regulations ensuring the safety of your investments against theft, loss, or fraud.

Rudra Associates partners with reliable depositories to ensure quick, transparent, and secure handling of your financial assets.

Portfolio Management Services (PMS) offer highly personalized investment management. This service is designed for clients seeking tailored investment strategies to meet specific financial goals, with an emphasis on optimal asset allocation, risk management, and long-term growth.

Service Features:

1. Discretionary PMS: Where the portfolio manager has full discretion to manage your portfolio based on pre-agreed goals and risk levels.
2. Non-Discretionary PMS: Clients are provided with investment recommendations that they can choose to act upon.
3. Customized Investment Strategies: Tailored strategies based on your unique financial goals, such as wealth creation, income generation, or tax planning.
4. Regular Performance Reviews: Monitoring and reporting of portfolio performance through comprehensive monthly or quarterly reports.

Our portfolio management team uses a data-driven approach, ensuring your investments are aligned with market trends, economic conditions, and your specific financial objectives.

Our IPO Distribution Services enable clients to participate in Initial Public Offerings (IPOs), giving them the chance to invest in companies as they go public. We provide timely alerts, detailed IPO analysis, and application facilities for a seamless process.

Service Features:

1. Easy Application Process: Simple and secure process to apply for IPOs, whether through online or offline channels.
2. IPO Research & Analysis: Access to detailed reports and analysis on upcoming IPOs, their financial health, business models, and market potential.
3. Timely Updates: Stay informed with updates on new IPO listings, their issue dates, and closing dates.
4. Post-IPO Advisory: Once the IPO is launched, we continue to offer expert advice on whether to hold or sell based on market conditions.

This service ensures you have access to high-potential IPOs and can apply seamlessly through our platform.

Fixed Deposits (FDs) are a conservative and secure investment option. We provide fixed deposits with a range of tenures, interest rates, and offerings from leading banks and financial institutions.

Service Features:

1. High-Interest Rates: We offer competitive interest rates, providing better returns compared to traditional savings accounts.
2. Flexible Tenures: Choose from flexible tenures ranging from a few months to several years, depending on your financial needs.
3. Tax-saving Fixed Deposits: We offer tax-saving FDs that help reduce taxable income, with the benefit of tax deductions under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
4. Interest Payout Options: Choose from monthly, quarterly, or lump sum interest payouts as per your preferences.

Rudra Associates partners with reputed banks and financial institutions to offer high-quality, risk-free fixed deposit solutions.

Mutual Funds are pooled investment vehicles that allow clients to invest in a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, and other assets. Rudra Associates helps clients navigate through the complex mutual fund landscape by offering a range of mutual fund options from leading fund houses.

Service Features:

1. Wide Range of Mutual Funds: Access to equity, debt, hybrid, sectoral, and international mutual funds based on your risk tolerance and investment goals.
2. Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs): Invest in mutual funds through SIPs, allowing clients to invest a fixed amount periodically and benefit from rupee cost averaging.
3. Tax-saving Mutual Funds (ELSS): Invest in tax-saving mutual funds that qualify for deductions under Section 80C.
4. Professional Fund Management: We provide access to funds managed by experienced fund managers who make investment decisions on behalf of investors.

Our mutual fund services help investors build long-term wealth while diversifying their portfolios through a range of investment options.
